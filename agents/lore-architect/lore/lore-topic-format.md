Lore topics are plain markdown files in `lore/`. No frontmatter.

- Filename: lowercase kebab-case, descriptive of the content
- Size: under 5K tokens preferred, can exceed when needed
- Content: atomic — one concept, decision, discovery, or recommendation
- Can reference other topics by filename
- Summary topics provide an overview of an area and link to related topics
- Only essential information — no filler or general knowledge anyone could look up
- Should include operational recommendations when relevant (e.g., "if you need to do X, do it through Y")
